Ariana Berlin fought to get back after a car accident

Ariana was in a car accident that resulted in two collapsed lungs, two broken legs, a broken collarbone, and a broken wrist. She was forced to retire from gymnstics and had to have 4 surgeries to repare the damage. She was in a lot of pain for a long time. She wasn't able to do gymnsatics and instead ended up breakdancing for a while to get back into the groove of things. Breakdancing is what brought her back to gymnastics.

Timeline of Ariana's life

  • October 29, 1987 -Ariana born
  • 2001 -Car Accident
  • She spent five days in a coma
  • Began Breakdancing as soon as she got out of the hospital
  • September 2005 -Back to gymnastics
  • 12 May 2018- Ariana marries Jimmy Rotstein
